Snapchat Fast Facts

History of Snapchat

Snapchat was founded in 2011 by three students at Stanford

Snapchat is launched as an app in the adroid store in 2012

In 2013 Snapchat was hacked, but it hasn't had a breach since

Snapchat (as a company) rebranded to Snap inc. in 2016

Users of Snapchat

There are 265 million daily active users of Snapchat

The average active Snapchat user opens the app 20 times per day

59% of people aged 13-24 use Snapchat

29% of people who have used Snapchat have a college education

How to Use Snapchat

The Various Features of Snapchat

Snapchat is very well known for it's ability to enable users to maintain "streaks" with other users. On top of that feature, it also has thousands of different filters, a story feature where you can share parts of your life with all of your friends at once, and an explore page where you can view content from other users. On top of sending images, Snapchat allows you to also share short videos that can also have filters applied to them and result in some of the funniest content.

Of course what has been mentioned above is only a small sampling of what Snapchat has to offer. Beyond what has already been mentioned it has features like its ability to chat with other users, snap memories, the snap map, geofilters, lenses, etc.
